ABOUT



FOUNDER OF SVIET

Sri Gudivada Ramachandra Rao

Great personalities engage themselves in a smooth metamorphosis as they move from success in one field towards success in various other fields. The success-filled lifestyle of Sri Gudivada Ramachandra Rao, our Founder Chairman, best exemplifies this fact. The saga of success began 60 years ago when he ventured into the Rice Mill Industry and carved a niche for himself as an Industrialist par Excellence. His successful journey elevated him to the post of President, Krishna District Rice Miller’s Association, where he served for 30 years. He was also the Past President of Lion’s Club, MTM.



CHAIRMAN OF SVIET

Sri Gudivada Meher Prasad

“Success is a journey, not a destination. It is not just an achievement, which is static, but an ever-ending pursuit of man.” Sri Gudivada Meher Prasad Garu, the Chairman of Sri Vasavi Institute of Engineering and Technology, personifies this pursuit and happiness.As a philanthropist and educationist, he dreamt of establishing an educational institute that would become a top-class technology institute, a pride of Machilipatnam, and Krishna district.With his leadership, SVIET aims to achieve excellence and provide quality education, nurturing students to become future leaders.



SECRETARY OF SVIET

Sri Tadepalli Meher Baba

Abdul Kalam once said, “Vision ignites the mind.” It is this vision that drives Sri T Meher Baba, our Secretary, to produce engineers who possess competence, conscience, compassion, and commitment. With an unwavering desire to achieve excellence, Sri Meher Baba, a renowned industrialist, philanthropist, and inspired educational visionary, is determined to make the college a center of learning that matches global standards.Serving as the Secretary of the Jaycees Club, MTM Chapter, and Vice-president of the Rice-Millers’ Association, MTM, Sri Meher Baba is an entrepreneur by choice and an eager educationist at heart.



CORRESPONDENT OF SVIET

Sri Kaky Kumar Babu

Riding high after reaching the peaks as an Engineer and counting on his 37 years of rich industrial exposure in L&T, India, and Texmaco Group of Companies in Indonesia, Sri K Kumar Babu is a man on a mission.Sri Kumar Babu understands the importance of nurturing a serene, intellectually-driven, and academically-rich environment. The sprawling campus, landscaped to perfection, and state-of-the-art infrastructural facilities are all a tribute to his working style. Modest to the core, he lets his work speak for itself. The aesthetic look of the campus stands as a testimony to his caliber and commitment.



EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR-1

Sri Tammana Sai Kumar

Sri Sai Kumar comes with a rich experience of 8 years in various prestigious engineering colleges and carries a cool head on his shoulders. He has pursued his M.Sc. in Mathematics from Andhra University and M.Phil. from Annamalai University. With him at the helm of affairs as the Executive Director, SVIET is bound to go on the path of success.With the aim of achieving excellence through educational leadership, Sri Tammana Sai Kumar, our Executive Director, is pioneering to fortify the engineering education to suit industry standards.



EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR-2

Sri Dosapati Baba

Sri Dosapati Baba, Executive Director, is a proactive personality who wishes to mold the minds of the students, bring about desired changes, and create a group of people dynamic in nature and versatile in stature. Sri Baba has done his M.Sc. and M.Phil. in Physics from Pondicherry Central University. With four years of experience as an Assistant Professor in Physics, he understands the needs of the student community, and his willingness to cater to their needs augurs well for the college.
